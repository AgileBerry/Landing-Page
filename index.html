Corgis are charming, energetic dogs with short legs, long bodies, and big, expressive eyes. Originally bred for herding in Wales, they're known for their intelligence, agility, and bold, confident personalities despite their small size. Corgis are incredibly affectionate and social, forming strong bonds with their families and always eager to be by your side. With their adorable, stubby legs, perky ears, and wagging tails, corgis have become beloved for their playful nature and irresistible cuteness, making them popular companions and internet stars alike.
